The Lodestar App

Amateur astronomy has been an endeavour experienced by many people across the centuries, and by people around the world. The astronomical objects that can be seen, or indeed positioned well enough to be seen, varies depending on geographical location around the world.

This web application is therefore able to extract the viewer's geographical coordinates, query a database, and create a ranked observing list of the brightest objects that can be seen from that very location..
